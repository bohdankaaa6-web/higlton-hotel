Getting Here

From Airport

Take the Airport Express train to Downtown Station (25 minutes), then a 5-minute taxi ride to the hotel. Airport shuttle service available upon request.

Public Transit

Subway: Lines 1, 2, 3 to Downtown Station (2-minute walk)
Bus: Routes 14, 22, 45 stop directly in front

Parking

Complimentary valet parking available for all guests. Secure underground self-parking also available. Electric vehicle charging stations on-site.
