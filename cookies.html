Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Higlton uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide reporting information.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function (e.g., keeping you logged in).
  • Performance cookies: To analyze how visitors use the site (via Google Analytics).
  • Preference cookies: To remember your language or currency settings.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose
Session Cookies Used to manage your booking process. Expire when you close the browser.
Analytics Help us understand traffic patterns and improve the UI/UX of Higlton.
Marketing Used to show relevant offers based on your interests.

4. How Can You Control Cookies?

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though some functionality may be restricted.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to the cookies we use for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
