Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which our website collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

1. Information Collection

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and booking details provided during registration.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and session duration.
  • Financial Info: Payments are processed via secure third-party gateways. We do not store your card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Collected Information

We may use the information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage your bookings.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To improve customer service and website functionality.
  • To send periodic emails regarding your orders or updates (with your consent).

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to enhance user experience, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic via Google Analytics.

4. Data Protection & GDPR

We implement SSL encryption and security best practices. If you are a resident of the EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data.

Refund Policy

1. General Terms

We strive to provide excellent service. If you need to cancel your booking, the following rules apply.

2. Cancellation Timeline

  • Full Refund: If cancelled at least 48 hours before the scheduled arrival.
  • Partial Refund: Cancellations made within 24-48 hours may incur a fee (e.g., the cost of the first night).
  • No Refund: For "No-shows" or cancellations made on the day of arrival.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us via email with your booking number. Requests are processed within 3-5 business days.

4. Transaction Fees

Please note that banking or payment system commissions are non-refundable.
